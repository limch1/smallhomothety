Speedtober 2024

Each year, the Hollow Knight Speedrunning Community hosts an event called Speedtober! This is an event of which showcases a lot of smaller meme categories that are somewhat popular, in order to encourage runners to try out some new categories that might be a little silly.

Speedtober is also a challenge for runners to try and complete a run every day for the entire month of October! Of course, you can participate in as many or as little days as you want.

This year, we're creating this webpage to show leaderboards and resources for each category for each day of the month! You can optionally submit your runs through the leaderboard form on this website, or you can just join our discord and talk about it in the daily threads!

Rules

Use the navigator above to navigate to the day that you want to speedrun for, then use the form to submit your time.

Please do not spam the forms! You will get one entry per day.

Speedruns

Select which ruleset you are going to run (NMG, 1432, NMMS or AG) and download the relevant splits from HKSplitMaker.

You don't need to record your run, but feel free to share your pb to the daily thread in #hk-community-events, and submit your time to the leaderboards via the navigator above.

ILs

Watch the linked example video at the top of each daily page, or find it in the discord discussion in #hk-community-events. Then, download the splits file, or if it's a single room, feel free to set the timer up yourself.

Once you're done, feel free to share your PB in discord or in the leaderboards on this site!

Rando/Plandos

For each week, players will have one week (or slightly longer at the end of the month) to complete a playthrough of the relevant rando. There are two categories, one for more "rando"-esque plandomizers, and one for harder, skip-focused plandomizers.

If you don't want to record your time, that's okay! You can put the time that appears on the stats screen in if you'd like, or just tick "completed" to be added to an honorary leaderboard.

Don't worry if you've already beaten the plando before, you're still allowed to compete! Just make sure you tick the "already played" option when you go to submit.

Instructions on how to set up plandomizers can be found here.
